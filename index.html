The art at the tip of the pencil

Art Hub Concept

Arthub Seeks to emphasize art made with hands on a canvas. Artists who use the “old” method of pencil, pen or brush, to transform canvas into pure art. We wish here to open space for artists who are inside their homes exposing their art only to themselves to participate in an active community that seeks to emphasize the artist and his beauty. We will put your skills in the form of pictures, so that an entire artistic class can have a broad view of your art and thus magnify everyone who is participating here. We respect all artists and their individualities, but our focus is on demonstrating people who paint pictures using pencils, pens or ink. Other types of art will not be accepted for display on our website.

What is drawing?

In fine art, the term "drawing" may be defined as the linear realization of visual objects, concepts, emotions, and fantasies, including symbols and even abstract forms. Drawing is a graphic art which is characterized by an emphasis on form or shape, rather than mass and colour as in painting.
